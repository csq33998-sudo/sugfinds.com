FAQ

MaisonLooks spreadsheet questions

Answers for shoppers using this page as a StreetStyle discovery hub.

What is the MaisonLooks Spreadsheet?

It is a curated discovery page for MaisonLooks StreetStyle finds, organized with category filters, QC notes, sizing checks, and W2C links.

Is this the checkout store?

No. This page helps shoppers discover and compare finds. Product browsing and checkout happen on MaisonLooks StreetStyle.

Is this an official Sugargoo spreadsheet?

No. This is an independent MaisonLooks StreetStyle discovery page for shoppers who search for Sugargoo spreadsheet-style finds, QC notes, and W2C links.

Why include QC and fit notes?

QC and fit notes help shoppers decide whether an item is worth opening, what details to inspect, and which sizing risks to consider.

How often should the list be refreshed?

Review the list weekly and refresh it whenever MaisonLooks adds strong new products, sells out items, or changes category priorities.
