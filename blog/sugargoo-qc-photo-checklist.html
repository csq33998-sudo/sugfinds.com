What to check before shipping a Sugargoo parcel

Updated June 2026 · 6 min read · Warehouse QC checklist

Warehouse photos are your best chance to catch sizing issues, color differences, poor stitching, or wrong variants before paying for international delivery.

Check the full shape first

Look at the item from a distance before zooming into details. For shoes, compare toe shape, heel height, and side profile. For clothing, check proportions, sleeve length, shoulder width, and overall cut.

Inspect details that sellers often miss

Review stitching, logo placement, zipper quality, tags, buttons, drawcords, embroidery density, print alignment, and visible glue marks. Small details are easier to catch before the item leaves the warehouse.

Use measurements when fit matters

If you are between sizes, request or review measurements. Insole length, chest width, shoulder width, waist, rise, and inseam can matter more than the size label shown on the listing.

Compare with the original product link

Keep the W2C link open while reviewing QC. Make sure the warehouse item matches the selected color, size, batch, and version from the seller page.

QC habit:

Save useful QC examples. They help you compare future finds faster and make your spreadsheet notes more accurate.
