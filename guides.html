How to check outerwear sizing

Compare shoulder width, chest, sleeve length, and total length. Leave room if the jacket will be layered over a hoodie.

How to review sneaker QC

Check toe shape, heel symmetry, tongue padding, sole height, and measured insole length if you are between sizes.

How to spot a useful streetwear find

Prioritize pieces that work in multiple outfits, have clear detail photos, and include enough sizing information.
